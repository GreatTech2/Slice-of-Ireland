There are a number of events that happen in the Fingal reign which are designed to celebrate their history and heritage as well as for charity, many of these events often take place in the town of Swords as it is the location of the county council. These can often take the form of gatherings with music and games other times they may be in the form of a market.


(image sourced from Fingal.ie)

Flavours of Fingal

Flavours of Fingal, Ireland’s largest family-friendly county show. Urban and rural communities get to see the best of Irish Agriculture and Food and learn all about where their food comes from. This year’s event is taking place on Saturday 1st and Sunday 2nd of July, at the Newbridge House and Farm, in North Dublin


Concerts in the Castle

There will be a number of great musical Concerts held at the 823 year old Swords Castle for people to enjoy be they locals or visitors they will be held from the 28th of July to the 30th of July.

Fingal Festival of Fire

Fingal Festival of Fire is an event that will take place in Swords, Malahide, Blanchardstown and Balbriggan. All in order to celebrate the hollyday of Halloween with some beautiful and amazing fireworks


Christmas Markets

Enjoy the holidays at the Christmas Markets held at Swords Castle on November 25th & 26th and December 2nd & 3rd, where the kids can have fun on the marry go round and enjoy the light show on display at the front of the Castle entrance.
