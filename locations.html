There are a number of locations that hold some form of significance to the town that are worth a visit when passing through the town of Swords whether that significance is tied to its historic connection to the town such as Swords Castle for example or is simply a popular place to travel to such as the finagle county council building. If visiting the town and looking for some food and drink Swords has a number of restaurants, pubs and clubs to choose from, many of which have their own interesting history to tell, in which you can relax and enjoy a nice meal either with friends or by yourself.
