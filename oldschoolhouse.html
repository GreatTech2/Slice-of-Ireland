Background

As the name suggest the old school house was orignaly build along the well road the year 1834 as a Protestant Boarding School and had cunnections to the Old Borough School. It remaind as such until the early 20th century when it was made into a childrens home and then after that was converted into a residence in the 1982.

Reputation

Since its conversion to a restront in the 1980s it has developed a refutation for providing a warm welcome to its patrends as well as providing friendly and efficient service to those that whish to eat there. It became so popluer that it was in the Fingal Accommodation & Dining Guide in 2003. It has sice become a fixture of the town and is know to by all that live withen the town even those that have never eaten there before.


Changes

Since its transformation into a pub it has gone through a number of changes and renovations. So much so that it is practiclay an intierlay new builing however still posses the original front of the old school house from when it was first constructed. Dispite this instead of trying ot go for a more modern look the pub attempes to retain the apperenc of an warm and holsom old pub.

Food

In terms of food the pub offets a wide number of meals to chose from, those inlcuded Starters like roasted wings smothered homemade School House BBQ sauce, they also offer tradiinal Irish Dishes such as Corned Beef & Cabbage, Fisherman’s Pie and finaly Beef & Guinness Pie. They have also experament with the serving of Mexican meals as well such as Spiced Chicken Enchilada, Chicken Fajita and Pulled Pork Burrito.

Entertainment

The pub also offer many forms of entertainment that are on during Thursdays, Fridays and Saturdays. For the most part this entertainment takes the form of live music preformed by profeinals as well as simpley those that have a talent for music. This music can change from day to day from traditional Irish music to more modern styles the next alowing for there to be someting for everyone to enjoy
